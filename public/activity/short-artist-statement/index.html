#texturalintercourse
Mixed media installation
RMIT University Gallery

#texturalintercourse is a magnificent, OTT, tongue in cheek, contemporary, psychedelic, psycho-sexual journey. A pop subversion exploring fetish in the everyday normality of life. It’s a mad world out there… Who knows what’s really on people’s minds or behind closed doors?… Interact with our piece and hashtag #texturalintercourse lovers.

Thanks to @honchodisko and all the beauties at the 200th Birthday who wrote on our toilet wall lining. We love you!
